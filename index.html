Hong Kong Style Milk Tea
Black tea with condensed or evaporated milk. For the most authentic taste, tea leaves should be filtered through a sackcloth bag, making the tea smoother.
Egg Puffs
Sweet egg batter cooked in a special egg-shaped waffle mold. A delicious street snack!
Pineapple Bun
A sweet bun with a crispy shortbread top. Contrary to its name, there is no pineapple flavor to this bun! The name comes from the pineapple-like appearance of its top.
Egg Tart
A custard tart with a flaky crust. A common variant is the Portuguese Egg Tart, which features a creme-brulee-esque caramelized top.
French Toast
Toast coated and fried in egg batter, slathered with peanut butter, and served with syrup and a dollop of butter on top.
Ham Macaroni
A breakfast staple, often served with a fried egg easy-over style, peas, carrots, and bok choy, spinach, or lettuce. Other options for meat include spam and sausage. An alternative involves instant noodle.
